# lite-wiki-note-slug

- built_from: `wiki_lite/RAW/...`
- status: `adopt | already-covered | hold | reject`
- surface: `memory | governance | skill | coordination`
- reviewed_at: `YYYY-MM-DD`
- claim_state: `fact | inference | opinion | hypothesis`
- freshness: `YYYY-MM-DD | range`
- confidence: `low | medium | high`
- confidence_basis: `explicit | conservative_default`
- scope: `valid scope and exceptions`
- source_count: `1 | unknown`
- source_count_basis: `trace_only | unknown`

## distilled

- Keep it lightweight and immediately usable.

## reusable rule

- Keep only reusable principles.

## next action

- Record whether the note should be promoted to canon or reviewed further.

## evidence

- Record the evidence location or source path.
- If only a single raw trace exists, treat it as `evidence_mode=trace`.
